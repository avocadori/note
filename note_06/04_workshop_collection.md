## プロンプト

```
A clean illustration of six worksheet/paper sheets laid out like a "personal product development kit."

Each sheet is a slightly different soft pastel tint, displayed at a soft angle (like fanned cards or scattered papers on a desk). Each sheet has:
- Several blank field rectangles where text would go (filled only with abstract dots and short lines, no actual letters)
- A small icon in the upper corner representing its theme (a person silhouette / a heart / a device / a coin / a megaphone / a growing plant)
- A subtle drop shadow giving them a tactile feel

A pen or pencil rests across one of the sheets, inviting the viewer to start writing.

Style: Flat vector illustration, modern Japanese editorial style,
soft pastel color palette (mint green #B8E0D2, soft pink #F4C7C3, sky blue #B5D8EB, cream beige #F5E8C7, warm gray #E0DDD3),
clean lines, organized and inviting mood,
white or very light background, plenty of negative space,
no text or letters in the image,
"a personal kit waiting to be filled" atmosphere.
```

## イメージの意図
6つのワークシートを「これから自分が埋める実用キット」として視覚化。
完成品ではなく「余白がある」状態を強調し、読者が手を動かしたくなる感覚を喚起する。
